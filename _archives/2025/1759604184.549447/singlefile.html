How Communities are Shaping and are Being Shaped By AI

Register here

Welcome to our event on How Communities are Shaping and are Being Shaped By AI! Join us on Thu May 29 2025 at 13:00 at the Taylor Institute for Teaching and Learning to explore the fascinating ways in which AI is impacting our communities. From healthcare to education, AI is revolutionizing the way we live and interact with each other. Come learn from experts in the field and engage in thought-provoking discussions on the future of AI and its role in shaping our communities. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to be part of the conversation!

​​​

Purpose:

To highlight community initiatives that engage with AI
